Our Aim

The IIT Patna Internship & Research Connect Portal is a student-driven initiative aimed at bridging the gap between juniors and experienced seniors or alumni. Our mission is to streamline the process of mentorship, referrals, and research exploration for IIT Patna students.

Why This Portal?

Navigating internships, research opportunities, and professional networking can be overwhelming, especially for juniors. This platform is built to simplify that journey by offering a centralized hub to:

  • 📨 Request referrals from trusted seniors and alumni for internships at top companies.
  • 🔬 Explore research projects posted by professors and senior students across departments.
  • 💬 Connect directly with seniors through our inbuilt messaging system for academic and career advice.
  • 📅 Track your referral requests and get notified when accepted or rejected.
  • 📂 Upload and showcase your GitHub & LinkedIn profiles to present your skills and achievements.
  • 🌐 Stay informed about the latest openings, workshops, and academic opportunities.

💡 Our Vision

We believe that students should not have to struggle to access the right guidance or opportunities. This portal ensures that support is just a click away. Whether you're a freshman looking for direction or a senior wanting to give back, this is your space.

Join us in building a more connected, helpful, and resourceful IIT Patna community. Let’s turn mentorship into a movement.
